Partha is an attempt to digitize the Bhagavad Gita and make it open and accessible to everyone

"I am a very short book.
But I can change your life.
I do not belong to any group of people.
I belong to no one, but all belong to me.
I was uttered by the Lord himself.
The great sage born on an island,
He committed me to letter.
The world is just an illusion, like a film
And I am the song,
The Song of God
The Bhagvad Gita"
